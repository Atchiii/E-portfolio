Introduction

This e-portfolio is a collection of my journey as an English Pre-Service Teacher from the College of Teacher Education at Western Mindanao State University. It captures the experiences I’ve had both on-campus and off-campus during my internship. Moments that challenged me, shaped me, and helped me grow into the kind of teacher I aspire to be.

Inside, you will find my weekly journals, reflections, documentation, and insights, all aligned with the Philippine Professional Standards for Teachers (PPST). These reflections show how I’ve grown in different areas throughout the internship. The six assigned readings are also included, along with my thoughts and takeaways from each one.

More than just requirements, this e-portfolio is a space where I’ve documented the lessons that truly mattered. Lessons about teaching, learning, and becoming a better version of myself. It shows not only what I have done but how I have changed. This is my way of showing that I am ready to step into the classroom with purpose, heart, and a deeper understanding of what it means to be a 21st-century educator.
